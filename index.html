About Us

KreditVenture, a pioneering NBFC, believes that the MSMEs are the actual backbone of India’s Industrial economy. While they contribute significantly to the country’s GDP, driving innovation & providing millions of livelihoods, we believe that they need to be empowered financially further.


Our innovative approach aims to reach out to the remote and underserved areas (Often ignored by traditional banks) and strengthen these micro enterprises with the help of Secured Business Loans (LAP) for their growth, while addressing a Total Accessible Market (TAM) of INR 20 Lac Crores, which has a penetration of less than 10%.
