Our Products

Unlock the potential of your property.

Our Secured Business Loans are designed to provide MSMEs with access to affordable capital by leveraging property as collateral. Whether it's to expand operations, invest in infrastructure, or manage working capital, these loans empower businesses to take the next big leap.

Documents Required

For Business / Self Employed

  • Aadhaar and PAN Card
  • Proof of Registration
  • 6 Months Sales-Purchase Bills
  • 1 Year Bank Statement
  • ITR / GST Return

For Salaried

  • Aadhaar and PAN Card
  • Minimum ₹15000 Salary
  • 3 Months Salary Slips
  • Form 16 (2 Years)
  • 1 Year Bank Statement

Why Submit These Documents?

Providing the required documents allows us to evaluate your financial standing accurately. This ensures that we can offer you the most suitable loan options tailored to your business needs.

Our aim is to support you in your entrepreneurial journey and help you achieve your business goals with the right financial backing.
