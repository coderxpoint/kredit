About Us

Transforming India’s Business Landscape

  • •

    Our vision lies in the promise of the extraordinary transformation of India in this Amrit Kaal. We see immense potential at the hands of small business owners of MSMEs, across the country.

    • •

      We believe in bridging the credit gap for these small entrepreneurs, to fully unleash their potential, which will lead to further strengthening of our nation’s economy. The inaccessibility to access and secure credit for their growth in rural areas, is something that we believe is the biggest hurdle for India becoming a developed nation.

Bridging The Credit Gap

The Singular Mission:

  • • Empowering small business owners by bridging the credit gap, with our tailored solutions.
  • • Ensuring financial inclusion to support the growth of Small Entrepreneurs, fueling their ambitions and driving growth and well-being of our country.

Empowering Rural Entrepreneurs

  • • Challenges faced by rural businesses and business owners go unnoticed despite some incredible success stories coming to light. Their constant battle for access credit is the primarily hurdle for them to succeed big.
  • • Our easy access financial solutions allow us to focus on thinking rural to see things distinctly as they are in rural set-ups, and solve these credit issues and enable unprecedented growth for these pillars of our nation’s economy.

Our Mission to Support MSME Growth and Success

  • •

    KreditVenture may be starting small, but our ambitions are anything but small. Our outreach program to the far corners of our country with the presence of local offices, helps us in making a strong connection with our clients. We learn everyday by being on the ground with them about their core challenges inn not just starting out, but also at every stage of their business operations.

  • •

    That is why we aim to help small business at every stage of their business journey, be it in Start-Up Capital, Operations Expansion or addressing a Geographic Growth Issue to expand business.

  • •

    Our learnings and experience are helping us solve many more challenges like accounting and retail business issues for small businesses, giving them an edge over their competition in the market. Our growth trajectory clearly enables us to be a strong regional player in 3-5 years from now and keep evolving with our clients by refining our solution each day for the betterment of MSMEs.

Meet Our Team

Saif Hasan

FOUNDER & CEO

Saif Hasan has more than 20 years of Sales and Strategic Alliances experience with Consumer Banks and a Private Wealth Firm. He was Director with IIFL Wealth. Prior to IIFL, he used to head Citibank consumer branches in Mumbai, leading large teams on Liabilities, Assets, Insurance, Wealth and Operations. He Co-Founded a Fintech startup KreditServe focused on B2B Supply Chain. Saif is a Post-Graduate in Business Economics (MBE) from University of Delhi and a Graduate in Commerce from BHU.

Jaspreet Singh

CO-FOUNDER

Jaspreet has over 16 years of experience and a proven track record in Transaction Banking Sales, Trade Finance and Supply Chain Finance (SCF) Product, Solution Advisory. He has led Product Sales teams across Large Multinational & Indian Banks like HSBC, SCB & Axis, Enterprise Sales stints for a large global fintech organizations-C2FO & setup the SCF and SME business for NBFC-Arthmate. Jaspreet holds a B Tech degree from DCE and an MBA from IIFT, Delhi.

Prajit Chandrasekhar

FOUNDING PARTNER

Prajit brings over 20 years of experience in corporate banking, investment banking, and fintech platforms. He previously served as the COO of Morgan Stanley’s Global Research Operations team in Mumbai and led operations at RXIL (TReDS platform). Prajit also founded his own tech startup in the Middle East and worked with a couple of new-age lending fintech startups contributing to business operations and product. He holds an MBA degree from the Indian School of Business (ISB).
